Home Remedies

Milk and Turmeric: Combine milk and turmeric to make a paste. Turmeric has anti-inflammatory qualities, while milk softens skin. After 20 minutes, wash off the paste that you applied to your face.

Aloe Vera: Fresh aloe vera gel from the plant should be applied to your face. Aloe vera moisturizes and calms skin. Rinse with cool water after leaving it on for ten to fifteen minutes.

Yoghourt with Cucumber: Blend cucumber and add to yoghurt mixture. Yoghurt gently exfoliates the skin, while cucumber cools and revitalizes it. After 15 minutes, wash your face after applying.

Papaya: Mash a ripe papaya and use it as a face mask. Enzymes found in papaya help to brighten and exfoliate skin. After 20 minutes, remove it with a warm water rinse.

Honey and Oatmeal: Mix oatmeal and honey to make a scrub. Honey hydrates and oatmeal exfoliates dead skin cells. Use this combination to gently scrub your face, then rinse it off with warm water.
